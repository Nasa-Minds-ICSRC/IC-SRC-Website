Who Are We?

Our club organizes research teams focused on developing robotic technologies to support space exploration for NASA competitions. We're always open to collaborating with other people to expand research opportunities across all fields!

Why Should You Join?

  • Apply academic skills to real-world projects
  • Collaborate with other people excited about changing the world
  • Learn about internship and scholarship opportunities through club collaborations and past alumni club members

How to Meet with Us

The club lab is in room 361 of the Biological Sciences Building at Cal State LA. We have weekly club meetings every Friday from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM. On Fridays, the lab is kept open for everyone to work in from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

When Are Team Meetings?

  • Cryo-UAV Team: Friday, 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Cryo-Bot Team: Friday, 12:00 PM – 2:30 PM
  • Cryo-Sense Team: Friday, 1:30 PM – 3:00 PM
